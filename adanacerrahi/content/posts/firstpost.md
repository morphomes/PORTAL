Welcome, 4th-year medical students, to your journey into **General Surgery**.

This course is designed to introduce you to the principles, techniques, and decision-making processes that define surgical practice. Our aim is not only to teach you the technical aspects of surgery but also to help you develop the clinical reasoning and professional mindset essential for patient care.

In this preface, you will find:
- The goals of the course
- Expectations for your participation
- Guidance on how to approach surgical cases
- Resources to support your learning

We hope this serves as a roadmap for your studies and inspires you to engage deeply with the material.
